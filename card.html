Llama

Llamas communicate by humming. They also express themselves through a series of ear, body, and tail postures.

  • Scientific Name: Llama glama
  • Average Length: 3 - 5 Feet
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: Andes Mountains

According to legend, when the Spaniards arrived to South America, they had never seen llamas before. They kept asking “¿Cómo se llama?” meaning, "what are they called?". So the Incans thought “llama” was the Spanish name.
